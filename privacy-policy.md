# Privacy Policy — Myco Tracker

Effective date: May 2026

Myco Tracker is designed to work primarily as a local offline application for culture, plate, slant, lineage, and workflow tracking.

## Information Collection

Myco Tracker does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

All strain data, plate records, lineage information, notes, and photos are stored locally on your device unless you explicitly export or share them yourself.

## Photos and Media

If you choose to add plate photos, those images are stored locally on your device and are only used inside the app.

## Internet Access

Myco Tracker may open external links when requested by the user, such as Instagram pages, Google Play Store pages, or future support links.

The app does not upload culture, plate, lineage, photo, or tracking data to external servers.

## Backups

The app includes optional export/import backup functionality. Backup files are created only when initiated by the user and are stored or shared using the device’s own file and sharing systems.

## Third-Party Services

The Android version of Myco Tracker is distributed through Google Play and may rely on standard Android and Google Play services.

## Data Security

Because data is stored locally on the device, users are responsible for securing their own device and exported backup files.

## Children’s Privacy

Myco Tracker is not directed toward children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in future versions of the app.
